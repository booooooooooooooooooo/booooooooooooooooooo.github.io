Hello!

My name is Gianluca Boo, I am research fellow at the WorldPop Unit, University of Southampton, currently supporting statistical modeling and mapping for the GRID3 project. Previously, I earned a PhD at the Department of Geography, University of Zurich, where I worked on a research project at the intersection between statistical modeling and public health. I also completed a postgraduate certificate in GIScience at the Spatial Information Laboratory, VU University Amsterdam, and both an MSc and BSc at the Institute of Geography and Sustainability (IGD) of the University of Lausanne. Parallel to my academic career, I worked as geoinformatician for a Swiss regional office and as urban planner/GIS manager for an NGO based in East Jerusalem.

My research interests lie in the area of spatial analysis, statistical modeling, and public health.
